Work process

Cosmetic dentistry

Our cosmetic dentistry process starts with a thorough consultation and digital smile design, followed by a tailored treatment plan (whitening, bonding, veneers or aligners), precise, minimally invasive procedures, and scheduled follow-up to ensure lasting, natural-looking results.

Pediatric dentistry

We create a child-friendly pediatric dentistry experience starting with a gentle exam and age-appropriate education, followed by preventive treatments (sealants, fluoride), comfortable restorations when needed, and regular recall visits to support lifelong healthy smiles.

Dental Implants

Our dental implant process starts with a detailed consultation and 3D imaging to design a personalized plan, followed by precise implant placement and a healing period for osseointegration, then abutment and natural-looking crown placement, plus scheduled follow-ups to ensure long-term function and comfort.

Painless Tooth Cavity Removing Treatment

Modern dentistry offers advanced tools that make cavity drilling almost pain-free treatment... The use of high-speed dental handpieces ensures minimal vibration and reduces discomfort. Local anesthesia is applied to numb the area, making the entire procedure smooth and painless. After cleaning the decayed portion, a safe and durable filling material is placed to restore tooth strength. Patients can return to normal eating habits within a few hours.

Professional Dental Cleaning Benefits

Routine dental cleaning removes stubborn plaque and tartar that brushing cannot eliminate... Dental hygienists use specialized tools to deep clean teeth and prevent gum infections. This procedure helps maintain fresh breath, reduces cavity risk, and improves overall oral hygiene. A professional cleaning every six months is recommended for everyone, especially those with sensitivity or stains.

Why Regular Dental Checkups Matter

Dental checkups help detect hidden oral issues before they become painful or expensive... Dentists inspect teeth, gums, bone structure, and soft tissues for signs of infection or decay. Early detection prevents tooth loss, gum disease, and oral cancer risks. A 6-month visit allows professional guidance on brushing habits, food choices, and preventive treatments.
